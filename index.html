SURFING

You will surf on some of the best surf spots in the world, always according to your prior surf experience. You won't miss any bit of the amazing North Shore's winter surf sessions. Where else in the world do you have the chance to see the top surfers of the planet competing on some of the largest and the most amazing waves on earth?

Aloha! Enjoy a little sun, a big beach, mornings full of surfing lessons, and evenings full of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at Scrap Shack Gym, located at the beginning of the 7-mile miracle on the North Shore of Oahu. During this camp, you will get to train Jiu-Jitsu with the Scrap Shack team and coaches and surf some of the best waves in the world with local surf instructors. All skill levels are welcome!

KAYAKING

We believe a child’s development is enhanced with exposure to a variety of sports at a young age. In line with our philosophy of sharing our passion for river running with our guests, we have developed 4-day kids’ kayaking camps that will help build paddling skills, confidence, and an appreciation for the beautiful area we live in.

All of our professional instructors love what they do, have a passion for running rivers, and love getting younger paddlers hooked on their favorite sport. We work with kids during our youth kayak program, as well as other well-known camps and youth groups.

WATER PARAGLIDING

Parasailing is one of the activities that is filled with fun and adventure. If you are witnessing individuals parasailing off the shore, you too might be dreaming about taking part in such an exciting activity. However, without knowing what to expect, this once-in-a-lifetime experience may bring fear instead of excitement. Fear and uncertainty are all normal feelings when you are not fully aware of the things to expect as you soar through the air.

Expect peak seasons to be fully booked most of the time. There will be a lot of parasailers who are also waiting to get their turn. On average, each parasailer spends one and a half hours in this activity. You know you are in good hands if the company you have booked the activity with takes their time to explain what is going to happen during the activity.
